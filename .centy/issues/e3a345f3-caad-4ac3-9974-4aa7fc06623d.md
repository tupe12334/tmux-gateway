# Make gRPC health check verify tmux availability

The gRPC health service (tonic-health) is configured in src/main.rs but only reports ‘serving’ after initialization. It doesn’t verify that tmux is actually running and accessible.

## Problem:

* Health check endpoint reports healthy even if tmux crashes after startup
* Docker/k8s health checks based on gRPC health won’t detect tmux failures
* The HTTP /health endpoint has the same limitation

## Solution:

* Implement a real health check that runs ‘tmux list-sessions’ (or similar) to verify tmux responsiveness
* Update the gRPC health reporter to reflect actual tmux status
* Consider periodic health checks vs on-demand checks
* Ensure health checks have their own timeout to avoid blocking
